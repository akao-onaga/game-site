## Features

- Real-time Reversi where every correct answer lets you place a piece
- Fast calculation can earn you multiple moves before the CPU acts
- Three CPU levels and three calculation difficulty levels
- Two-player mode with a dedicated mobile layout

## How to Play

- **Mouse / Touch**: Select a legal square, use the on-screen keypad, and confirm or erase
- **Keyboard**: Enter an answer with number keys, erase with Backspace, and confirm with Enter
- **Single player**: Select a legal square and solve the displayed equation to place a piece
- **Two players**: Player 1 taps the left board and Player 2 taps the right board, then both solve simultaneously on separate keypads

The player with more pieces wins when all 64 squares are filled or neither player has a legal move.
