## Features

- A 3D time attack built around carrying a tray of nitroglycerin
- Careful walking reduces speed and steadies the tray
- Fans, rotating bars, moving carts, slippery floors, and other hazards
- Five-second shortcut gates and an online time leaderboard

## How to Play

- **Move**: WASD / Arrow keys
- **Careful walk**: Hold Space or Shift while moving
- **Restart from the beginning**: R
- **Return to title**: Esc

The bottle explodes when the shake gauge reaches its limit. Each green gate reduces your time by five seconds the first time you pass through it.
