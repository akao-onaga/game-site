## Features

- Brain-training typing where A becomes Z, B becomes Y, and so on
- Online leaderboard

## How to Play

Enter a player name on the title screen, then select START.

Type the letters shown on screen. Each alphabet key is interpreted as its opposite: A becomes Z, B becomes Y, and so on.
