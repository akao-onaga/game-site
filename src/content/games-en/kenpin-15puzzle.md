## Features

- A new take on the 15 puzzle: determine whether each board is solvable
- EASY boards are generated only a few moves away from completion
- CRAZY boards are completely random
- Online time-attack leaderboard

## How to Play

- **Mouse / Tap**: Select buttons and move tiles
- **Ready to Ship**: Choose this when you think the board is solvable
- **Defective**: Choose this when you think the board is impossible

Inspect ten boards correctly in a row to clear the game. Your time and mistake count appear on the results screen.
