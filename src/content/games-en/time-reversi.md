## Features

- Real-time Reversi with a three-second cooldown between moves
- Practice against the CPU or play a friend with a room ID
- Survival mode against CPU opponents that grow stronger after every win
- Skills such as Free Stone, which ignores the normal cooldown
- Match replays for reviewing each game

A fast, new take on Reversi designed for short play sessions. Free to download with in-app purchases. Supports English and Japanese.
